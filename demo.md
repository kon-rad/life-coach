• App Summary

  Live Coach is an iOS accountability coach built around a very narrow habit loop: one goal, two daily check-ins, three micro-actions, and one score.
  Instead of acting like a generic chatbot or journal, it pushes the user to focus on a single meaningful project, plans the day with a short morning
  voice check-in, tracks completion of three small actions, and closes the loop with an evening reflection and score.

  What makes it distinct is the combination of:

  - Voice-first coaching for morning and evening check-ins
  - A simple dashboard showing score, streak, micro-actions, and progress
  - Ongoing chat and conversation history
  - Privacy positioning built into the product, with encrypted goals, sessions, and conversations stored through a proxy/backend layer rather than as
    plain text

  So the core pitch is: a private AI coach that helps you consistently move one important life goal forward every day.
